Defeat the Crystal Sage

You have entered the fog door, ashen one.


You have four sorcories to use. By clicking each sorcery you will add a specific amount of damage to your total score.


You defeat the Crystal Sage by matching your total damage with his total health. You will be defeated if your damage goes above his health.


The value of each sorcery is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
